Our History

Pioneering Exotic Medicine

For over two decades, ExoticVet has stood at the intersection of private veterinary practice and zoological conservation.

What began as a specialized avian clinic has evolved into a comprehensive, multi-disciplinary medical center treating everything from microscopic amphibians to large exotic mammals.

We are driven by the understanding that exotic pets require medical protocols vastly different from traditional domestic animals.
