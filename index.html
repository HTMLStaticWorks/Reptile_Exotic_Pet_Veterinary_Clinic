Medication & Support

In-House Exotic Pharmacy

Standard veterinary medications are rarely suitable for exotic species. Our specialized on-site pharmacy is equipped to provide highly specific formulations and dosages tailored precisely to the unique metabolic rates of reptiles, birds, and small mammals.

  • Custom Compounding

    We create palatable liquid suspensions for animals that refuse pills.

  • Species-Specific Dosages

    Micro-dosing capabilities for patients weighing only a few grams.

  • Emergency Stock

    Immediate access to life-saving antivenoms and specialized antibiotics.
