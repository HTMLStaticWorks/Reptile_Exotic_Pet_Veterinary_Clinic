Frequently Asked Questions

Do you handle after-hours wildlife emergencies?

Yes, we have a 24/7 specialist availability protocol. However, we ask that you call our emergency hotline (1-800-EXO-VETS) before arriving so our team can prepare the specific critical care incubator or antivenom required for your species.

Are specialist fees higher than regular vet visits?

Because our physicians are board-certified and utilize highly specialized micro-surgical equipment, our baseline consultation fees are higher than a standard dog/cat clinic. However, we provide transparent estimates before any diagnostics are run.

Do you offer Telemedicine for habitat reviews?

Absolutely. We highly recommend our virtual habitat consultations. Using video chat, our technicians can evaluate your enclosure's thermal gradients, humidity setups, and UV lighting placement from the comfort of your home.

Do you accept exotic pet insurance?

Yes, we are in-network with Nationwide Exotic Pet Insurance and several other specialized providers. Please bring your policy documentation to your first visit.
